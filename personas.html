Layers: context capability psychological behavior (mandatory, 3-5 active). Origins: inferred = supported by the safe task anchor; sampled = synthetic latent value compatible with this video; synthetic ctx = invented context. Grades A/B/C/O per §3.
Read the green badges with these caveats
  • The gates are provisional. The spec itself says they are "to be calibrated after the human pilot" — the 0.35 / 0.70 / 0.85 thresholds are educated guesses, so passing them is not yet evidence of quality.
  • The LLM critic scores 0.995 on average — treat that as suspect. A model critic that almost never objects is likely rubber-stamping. Its error rate is unmeasured; that is exactly what the 100-video human pilot (§13.4) exists to establish. The human pilot has NOT been run (it needs annotators + inter-annotator agreement).
  • All 5 demo videos are solo_task, so 5 social keys (extraversion, social_setting, social_risk_attitude, relatedness_support_preference, communication_accommodation) never activate. The social side of the bank is untested here.
  • The VLM emitted no visual_ambiguity, uncertain_outcome or time_pressure tags on any of the 5 videos, so uncertainty-related keys are underexercised (intolerance_of_uncertainty activated only 6/40 times).
  • Diversity is measured on the structure, not the prose: free-text keys are excluded from the Gower distance, otherwise differing wording would fake diversity (§15.8).
